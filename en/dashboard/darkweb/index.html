What should I add?

The more you register, the more comprehensive your protection. We recommend these as a minimum:

1

All email addresses you use regularly — especially those linked to banking, social media, or government services.

2

Your primary phone number — commonly used in SIM-swap attacks and targeted phishing campaigns.

3

Your SIN — the most sensitive piece of Canadian identity and a primary target for tax fraud.

4

Credit card and bank account numbers — frequently traded on dark web markets following large retail data breaches.

How it works

How Dark Web Monitoring protects you

ID Assist continuously scans thousands of dark web sites, criminal forums, and compromised data repositories for your personal information — and alerts you the moment anything is found.

1

You register what to protect

Add your email addresses, phone numbers, SIN, and other personal information to your monitoring profile. Each category has its own limit — your plan covers up to 50 items in total across all types.

2

We scan continuously

Our systems monitor thousands of dark web marketplaces, breach databases, hacker forums, and leaked datasets around the clock — checking every day for your registered information.

3

You're alerted immediately

If your information is detected, we notify you right away with details on what was found, where it appeared, and clear steps you can take to protect yourself. View alerts from the Alerts page.

If you receive a breach alert

What to do when your information is found

Don't panic — having your information detected is serious, but acting quickly and methodically dramatically reduces the risk of harm.

01

Change affected passwords immediately

If a password is found with your email, update it right away — and change it on any other site where you've used the same credentials.

02

Enable two-factor authentication

Add 2FA to any accounts linked to the exposed information. This prevents access even if a criminal already has your password.

03

Monitor your accounts and credit

Regularly review your bank statements and watch for credit alerts from us.

04

Contact our recovery team

If you suspect active fraud, call our 24/7 restoration line at 1-877-900-1837. Our experts will guide you through the next steps of restoring your identity.
