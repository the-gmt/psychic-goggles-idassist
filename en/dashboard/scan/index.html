What Scan & Score checks for
Our system analyses content across multiple threat categories.
Phishing emails
Deceptive messages posing as legitimate organizations to steal credentials or personal info.
Smishing texts
Fraudulent SMS messages designed to trick you into clicking malicious links or sharing information.
Malicious URLs
Website links that lead to fake pages, malware downloads, or other harmful destinations.
Screenshots
Images of emails, texts, or web pages — our AI extracts and analyses the content automatically.
Impersonation
Messages pretending to be from government agencies, banks, couriers, or well-known brands.
Social engineering
Urgency tactics, fear-based language, and manipulation techniques used to bypass your judgement.

Tips for best results
Include the full message. The more text or image content you provide, the more accurate the assessment.
Don't click links. Copy and paste the URL directly into the text field rather than clicking through.
Add sender details. If you know who sent the message, include their name and contact info — it helps us verify their identity.
Screenshots work too. A clear screenshot of the message gives us everything we need for a complete analysis.
