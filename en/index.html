How a data breach becomes your problem

Every breach follows the same pattern. Here's how ID Assist fights back when your information is at risk.

A company gets breached

Billions of records are exposed every year through breaches at retailers, telecoms, social media platforms, and government agencies.

Your information hits the dark web

Stolen emails, passwords, credit card numbers, and SINs get traded on hidden criminal marketplaces – often within days.

How ID Assist helps

Dark web monitoring – We watch for up to 50 pieces of your personal information across criminal marketplaces and quickly alert you when something is found so you can change passwords or cancel compromised bank cards to protect your accounts.

Scammers target you directly

Using your personal details, criminals send convincing emails and texts designed to trick you into giving up even more.

How ID Assist helps

Scan & Score – AI-powered scam detection analyzes any suspicious message and tells you instantly whether it's a threat.

Someone applies for credit in your name

Identity thieves use stolen information to open accounts or take out loans – leaving you with the damage.

How ID Assist helps

Dual-bureau credit monitoring – We monitor both Equifax and TransUnion daily. Most services only watch one – meaning they can miss the early signs of fraud.

Recovering your identity is complicated – but it doesn't have to be

Restoring your identity after theft can mean dealing with banks, credit bureaus, government agencies, and creditors – often all at once.

How ID Assist helps

Full-service identity restoration – Our specialists guide you through every step of recovery, including Limited Power of Attorney support – so you can focus on your life, not the paperwork.
